### Tank
**Key Traits**: Aggro management
**Common Pain Points**: ['Hard to manage multiple mobs']
**Goals**: ['Hold aggro on all enemies']
**Wishes Others Understood**: Wait for me to grab aggro before attacking!
**Edge Cases**: I can’t tank if I can’t move.
**Weapons**: Shield; Greatsword
**Preferred Games**: Guild Wars 2; WoW

### Healer
**Key Traits**: Resource management
**Common Pain Points**: ['Hates unpredictable damage', 'Needs safe zones']
**Goals**: ['Keep team alive', 'Support DPS']
**Wishes Others Understood**: DPS, don’t pull extra mobs—I can’t heal through that!
**Edge Cases**: I can’t heal if the tank won’t hold aggro.
**Weapons**: Scepter; Staff
**Preferred Games**: WoW; Guild Wars 2

### Hybrid
**Key Traits**: Jack of all trades
**Common Pain Points**: ['Gear is expensive']
**Goals**: ['Support when needed']
**Wishes Others Understood**: I can adapt, but I need clear communication.
**Edge Cases**: I need to switch roles often.
**Weapons**: Shield; Mace
**Preferred Games**: FFXIV; Guild Wars 2

### Physical Melee
